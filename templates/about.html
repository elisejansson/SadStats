About the Data Set

Processing

  • Years between 1999 and 2002 were removed during pre-processing to provide a tighter data set
  • Column 113_cause_name removed during pre-processing as it provided redundant information
  • Items with a death count in the range [0, 20) have a not applicable age adjusted death rate

Please note that items involving totals, such as 'All Causes' and 'United States', were not included in pivot table calculations

Definitions

Year - Specific year, spanning from 2003 until 2013

Cause Name - Specific cause, within the top 10 leading causes of death in the United States over the specified range of years

State - Specific state of residence in the specified year, within the United States

Deaths - Number of deaths, in the specified state and time period

Age Adjusted Death Rate - Rate that would have existed if the population under study had the same age distribution as the "standard" population, a summary measure adjusted for differences in age distributions.

Source

Age-adjusted death rates for the top 10 leading causes of death in the United States, including mortality patterns from 1999 through 2013, and by state of residence for selected year and cause of death.

This data set was accessed from data.gov, the home of the United States Government's open data.
